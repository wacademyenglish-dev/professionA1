Grammar: A vs An

To say your profession in English, you MUST use "a" or "an" before the job.

I am a server. (Starts with Consonant)

I am an actor. (Starts with Vowel)

I am doctor. (Wrong!)

Teacher: Explain that in Portuguese we say "Sou médico", mas em Inglês dizemos "Sou UM médico" (I am a doctor).

Grammar: Places of Work

To say the company or specific place you work for, use "AT".

She works at a hospital.

I work on Starbucks. (Wrong!)
